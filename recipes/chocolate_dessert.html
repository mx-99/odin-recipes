Super sweet easy to make chocolate dessert and you don't even need an oven

Source

    Ingredients

  • agar agar powder
  • cocoa powder
  • sugar
  • dark chocolate
  • milk

    Steps

  1. first add a pot 2½ cup of milk
  2. ½ cup of sugar
  3. 1 and halfe tablespoon of agar agar powder
  4. 1 and halfe tablespoon of cocoa powder
  5. give a good on medium low heat
  6. heat it up for 3 minutes
  7. After 3 minutes take off the heat
  8. Add 4 and half ounces of dark chocolate
  9. Throw it back on stove and continue mixing
  10. When it start boiling take it off the heat
  11. Place any kind of mold you have
  12. Place in refrigerator 1-2 hours

Mango lassi is a refreshing yogurt drink
very popular in hot summer days of Pakistan

  • 1 Mango
  • Yogurt
  • salt
  • ice cubes
  • sugar

Steps

  1. slice a mango add to your blender along with some yogurt
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar and a pinch of salt
  3. Add some ice cubes
  4. Add 1 cup of water and then blend away
  5. pour into your mug top it of with some cream mangos and garnish it with some mint
  6. Enjoy
